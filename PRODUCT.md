# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Planner Studio primarily serves creators producing complete planner products for customers. They need to define an audience and outcome, assemble a coherent multi-page set, customize every page, and prepare a polished deliverable without manually editing JSON coordinates.

## Product Purpose

Planner Studio turns the existing specification-driven generator into a guided visual workflow. It recommends a page blueprint from a curated pattern library, lets creators add, remove, replace, reorder, and edit pages, and supports snap-to-grid movement of blocks within validated layouts. Success means a creator can move from a customer brief to a complete, reviewed, export-ready planner set while retaining control over every page.

## Positioning

Professional multi-page planner creation without raw JSON or manual coordinates, combining guided structure with creator control.

## Brand Personality

Professional and creative. The interface should feel capable and purposeful while leaving room for thoughtful experimentation and distinctive customer work.

## Anti-references

The product must not feel generic. Avoid anonymous AI-generator styling, interchangeable dashboard decoration, unexplained automatic choices, and template-first experiences that erase the creator's judgment.

## Design Principles

- Recommend with reasons; never silently decide the product for the creator.
- Keep the whole planner coherent while preserving control over every page.
- Make valid layout behavior visual, immediate, and forgiving.
- Reveal complexity progressively instead of requiring raw coordinates or JSON.
- Treat publication readiness, accessibility, and customer fit as part of creation rather than final cleanup.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Support complete keyboard operation, strong text and control contrast, visible focus states, screen-reader labels and live status announcements, reduced-motion preferences, and interaction patterns that do not depend on color or pointer precision alone.
